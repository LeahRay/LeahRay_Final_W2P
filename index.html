Pop-ups have always been a functional and promotional presence, but in this exhibition, pop-ups become, in a poetic way, a slice in the chain of knowledge.

Digital Slice exhibition presents the whole thing by combining different pop-ups with slices of the same picture content. In "Digital Slice," pop-ups are reimagined as layers that, when combined, can present a comprehensive picture of a theme or story. Each popup is a slice of information, and it is only by exploring all the layers that the visitor can get the full picture. This exhibition seeks to transform pop-ups from mere digital distractions into meaningful containers of layered knowledge.

Uncover the stories hidden within the layers and piece together the complete images as you explore the exhibition that redefines the digital experience.
